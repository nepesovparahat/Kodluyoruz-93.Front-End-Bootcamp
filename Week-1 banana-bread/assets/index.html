Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

  • Serves 8
    • |
  • Prep 5 min
    • |
  • Cook 20 min
    • |
  • Ready in 25 min

A great recipe I found in an old recipe book.

Judging from the weathered look of this recipe card, this was family favorite.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose folur
  • 3 1/2 teaspoonus baking powder
  • 1 teaspon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

DIRECTIONS

  1. In large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approxismately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

NOTES

Aluminum folil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
